Project Overview

Project Description: Introducing the "Sembang bersama Puan Sundari, Pulih dari Strok" documentary, a project dedicated to showcasing the remarkable stories and experiences of individual who have overcome challenges and emerged stronger with the help of my client services and products. Through this series of testimonial videos, we aim to inspire and connect with our audience on a deep and personal level.

Objective: The primary objective of the "Sembang bersama Puan Sundari, Pulih dari Strok" documentary is to shed light on the resilience and triumph of the human spirit. Each scene will feature a compelling personal story, highlighting moments of struggle, growth, and ultimate success, with the aim of motivating and uplifting viewers.

Style and Tone: The video will adopt a documentary style approach to create a informative and emotional experience for the audience. Consistency in style and tone will contribute to the overall cohesiveness of the video.

Timeline: The project timeline is as follows:

Production/Shooting: [8 December 2022]

Post-production/Editing: [10 December 2022 - 12 December 2022]

Final Delivery: [13 December 2022]

Regular check-ins and progress updates will ensure that the project stays on track and aligns with our vision.

Conclusion: This project aims to deliver a compelling and visually stunning video that effectively communicates the key messages to the target audience. Collaboration, creativity, and attention to detail will be the guiding principles throughout the project lifecycle.
