Project Overview

Objective: The main goal of this project is to testimonial after the patients have good result. this is to ensure another potential patients to trust my client. Whether it's promoting a product, capturing an event, or telling a compelling story, the aim is to building trust.

Target Audience: The intended audience for this video includes potential costumers and online vievers. Understanding the audience helps tailor the content to resonate with their interests and preferences.

Key Messages: The key messages to be conveyed through this video are a strok patient process to heal by using my client service and product. These messages should align with the overall goals of the project and contribute to its success.

Style and Tone: The video will adopt a documentary style approach to create a informative and emotional experience for the audience. Consistency in style and tone will contribute to the overall cohesiveness of the video.

Timeline: The project timeline is as follows:
